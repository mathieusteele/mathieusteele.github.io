Site Name

The name I have chosen for my weather site project is PlayParisPeak.com. Paris Peak is the name of a mountain centrally located between the three cities Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. The domain name PlayParisPeak.com is available at the time of this writing. The annual domain name registration cost at google is $12.00.

Site Purpose

There are many reasons why people would visit a local community site. This project aims to showcase local cultural and social events, parks and outdoor activities, as well as featuring the five day weather forecast for the community members to enjoy. It is helpful to local residents as well as tourists who may be visiting the area.
