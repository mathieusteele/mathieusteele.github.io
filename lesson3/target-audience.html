Target Audience

This community site is being created as a resource for the southeast idaho region, particularly the towns of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Local residents and guests will utilize the site for local-interest information about events, service projects, and educational or cultural programs.

Persona 1: Outdoor Adventurer

After a successful career I have now retired and travel the country looking for the next thrill. I might find and utilize the features on this site for one of these reasons:

  • What campgrounds are available for Recreational vehicle access near Bear Lake?
  • Where can I get my non-resident fishing license?
  • What is the difficulty of the Paris Peak trail and Is there a map?
  • What temperatures can I expect thoughout the day on my hike?

Persona 2: Cabin Fever Parent

With three kids at home, I'm going nuts with cabin fever. I might turn to PlayParisPeak.com to find details on upcoming events, concerts, parks & playgrounds, and other experiences I can have with my family.

  • What bands are playing this year in the summer concert series?
  • When is the annual spring cleaning yard sale?
  • Who won the blue ribbon for best tomatoes in the fair last year?
