Bloomfield

History

The area now known as Bloomfield was acquired in 1788 as part of the Phelps and Gorham purchase. In 1789 the town was created as Bloomfield. In 1990, two neighboring communities, East Bloomfield, and Holcomb incorporated to form modern-day Bloomfield. Bloomfield is famous for the Northern Spy Apple, which originated in Bloomfield.

Bloomfield Today

Modern-day Bloomfield boasts a friendly atmosphere with a balance of farmland, small businesses, and residential neighborhoods. Many of the residents work in neighboring towns within Ontario County or in the Greater Rochester Area. There are many vibrant religious communities and service organizations including Rotary International and the Lyons club.
