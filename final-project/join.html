Membership Levels

  • Non-profit - Includes a directory listing and up to three social media posts per year.
  • Bronze - Includes a directory listing, up to five social media posts per year, 2 tickets to the luncheon, and a simple advertising package.
  • Silver - Includes a directory listing, up to 8 social media posts per year, 4 tickets to the luncheon, and a decent advertising package. Includes basic business training.
  • Gold - Includes a directory listing, up to 2 social media posts per month, one table for up to 8 people at the luncheon, and our most robust advertising package. Includes comprehensive business training.
