Best bait for spring fishing season

I've been fishing in Bear Lake since I was old enough to hold a pole. The appetite of the fish tends to change with the seasons. In all of my years on the water, I've never had better luck in the spring than when I use worms as bait. Sure, some people use other lures, or fancy new ways of attracting fish, but believe me when I say that I've tried everything and nothing gets the fish biting faster than real worms.
